We Are Wanderers

The idea of WANDERING or TRAVELLING has evolved over the years. As a result, it has acquired different meaning and significance. 

If we go back to the early historic times, wandering was associated with survival. The early humans did not have a stable life and were required to move around from one place to another in search of food, shelter and safety.

With gradual development, human beings started settling down and believed in the idea of having community, shared values and developing a culture out of it.
